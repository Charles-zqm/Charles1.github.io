    There’s nothing old when it comes to the Vans Old Skool, except how long this iconic skate shoe has been in style. One of the most popular models of all time from the famous California-based skateboarding and extreme sports shoe brand, the Old Skool has far surpassed its original status as a shoe designed for skateboarding to become a modern casual sneaker favorite for many. From skateboarders to fashionistas, the Vans Old Skool is loved for its clean, low-profile design, durability, and great comfort straight out of the box. The classic vulcanized Waffle sole from Vans offers excellent grip on or off the board, and a clean and simple no-nonsense style. The Old Skool is available in a wide variety of color and materials combinations, including all-canvas, all-leather, and graphic-printed options.
